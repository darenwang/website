Daren Wang

I am a Postdoc Scholar at the Statistics Department at the University of Chicago. My supervisor is Prof. Rebecca Willett. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in statistics at Carnegie Mellon University in August 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Alessandro Rinaldo and Prof. Christopher Genovese.


Education

  • Ph.D. in Statistics, Carnegie Mellon University (2018).
  • M.S. in Mathematics, University of Michigan (2011).
  • B.S. in Mathematics, University of Virginia (2010).

Research Interests
  • High-dimensional inference
  • Network models
  • Change point detection and time series
  • Functional data analysis
